FAQs

What is Honda Aviator?
Honda Aviator is a website that provide a greener and efficient way for people to get their ordered products.
How do I use Honda Aviator?
To use Honda Aviator, you will need to put in your destination after selecting a product into your cart, and through Honda Aviator, a drone will deliver that product. More information is available on the Homepage page.
Who should use Honda Aviator?
Anyone! This website was specifically designed for anyone to use as efficient and greener delivery should be a priority.
How do I contact Honda Aviator?
You can contact us through email or phone. More information is on the Contact Us page.
What if my package/delivery never arrived?
If you do not get your packcage, please contact us as soon as possible to get this issue sorted. Our contact information in on the Contact Us page.
How do businesses add in their products?
Businesses have a database page where they can upload their products by answering a few questions, and this would allow the user to view these products.
